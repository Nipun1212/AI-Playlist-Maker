Features

User Authentication

Users can securely log into their Spotify accounts within the web app, providing a personalized experience.

Music Recommendations

Based on the user's input of songs and artists, our AI-powered recommendation system, powered by ChatGPT and BARD, generates personalized music recommendations tailored to the user's taste and preferences.

NLP Techniques

Our web app applies natural language processing (NLP) techniques to extract relevant songs and artists from the generated recommendations, ensuring accurate and meaningful results.

Automatic Playlist Creation

Using the identified songs and artists, our web app automatically creates a personalized playlist on the user's Spotify account, saving time and effort.
